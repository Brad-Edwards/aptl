**Per-function complexity gate now covers the TypeScript surfaces.** A hard,
repo-wide ESLint `complexity` gate (cyclomatic, threshold 15 — the sibling of
the Python `ruff` `C901` gate) runs in the required *Pre-commit hooks* CI job
and locally, failing any new function over the threshold under `mcp/*/src/` or
`web/src/`, including functions in Svelte `<script lang="ts">` blocks. The
threshold, scope, and the three pre-existing exempted offenders all live in the
root `eslint.config.js`; see ADR-010.
