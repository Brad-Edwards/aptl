Removed three always-empty fields (`rendered_configs`, `evidence_paths`, `telemetry_paths`) from the ACES `NodeRealization` and its `details()`: they were populated by a misdirected runtime extractor that read `payload`/`spec` top-level `runtime`/`aptl` keys, whereas the real compiled ACES payload nests its runtime model at `spec.node.runtime` and never emits those field names. The fields were therefore always empty for real scenarios and consumed by nothing; the misdirected `runtime_spec`/`string_list` helpers are removed with them.
