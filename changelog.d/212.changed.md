**Scenario query helpers now carry `icontract` guards.** `aptl.core.sdl.Scenario` gains stable accessor methods (`get_objective`, `get_workflow`, `get_workflow_step`) with `icontract.require` / `icontract.ensure` decorators that fail fast on unknown names and assert the returned object belongs to the same scenario. The wrappers force `enabled=True` (survive `python -O`) and use a fixed-message `error=` factory so a violation never interpolates the bound `Scenario`'s `repr()` past the boundary. Structural and semantic SDL validation remain in Pydantic and `SemanticValidator` — contracts are not a third schema layer (ADR-014 §"Contract Guards").
