**Adopted [`towncrier`](https://towncrier.readthedocs.io/) for changelog management.** PRs no longer hand-edit `CHANGELOG.md`; they drop a small fragment under `changelog.d/<issue>.<type>.md`, and the release process collates fragments into `CHANGELOG.md` via `uvx towncrier build`. This avoids cross-PR changelog conflicts and includes `.gitattributes` `CHANGELOG.md merge=union` as additional protection for release-collation commits and straggler hand-edits.
