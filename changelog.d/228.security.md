### Security

**Removed red→SIEM pipe under the non-contamination principle (ADR-033).**
The Kali container no longer ships a Wazuh agent, no longer forwards
rsyslog to the Wazuh manager, and no longer sources the
`redteam_logging.sh` syslog helpers. The Wazuh manager no longer
loads `kali_redteam_rules.xml` or `kali_decoders.xml`, and
`mcp-red`'s OCSF logger no longer dispatches records to a SIEM sink.
Red activity captured by the platform now lives only in the
per-scenario experimental record under `.aptl/runs/<run_id>/`, where
blue's perception layer cannot see it. This eliminates an artificial
information leak that inflated blue-agent awareness in purple-loop
runs.

A new opt-out — `APTL_EXPERIMENT_NO_REDACT=1` on the MCP server's
environment — lets experimenters preserve credentials/secrets
verbatim in the per-run record when the credential IS the
experimental signal. The toggle defaults off and fails closed
against any non-truthy value; pcap content is always raw bytes by
design and unaffected.
