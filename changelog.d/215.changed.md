**MCP SSH session lifecycle now has explicit contract guards.** `PersistentSession.executeCommand`, `SSHConnectionManager.createSession`, `executeInSession`, and `getSessionOutput` enforce their preconditions through a single `require_` helper that throws `SSHError`. `PersistentSession.close()` and `SSHConnectionManager.closeSession` / `disconnectAll()` assert cleanup postconditions after every teardown so a future regression cannot silently drop a `clearTimeout` or `reject` step. Event-handler and timer-callback teardown paths (`stream.on('close')`, `resetSessionTimeout`) log loudly but cannot crash the Node process on a postcondition violation. No public API change.
