Fixed the TechVault fileshare scenario's planted SSH deploy key never being
created: the fileshare image was missing `openssh-client`, so the
`setup-shares.sh` keygen (which swallowed errors with `2>/dev/null || true`)
silently no-oped and the documented leaked-credential attack path in the
`it-backups` share did not exist. The image now installs `openssh-client` and
the keygen is idempotent and fails loudly; the fileshare inventory records the
now-present `deploy_key` / `deploy_key.pub` with their stat and sha256.
